NULLEXISTENCE

Stellar Frequencies – Spectrogram Challenge

Author: Arveen (Neevra_Llig)

Category: Steganography

Objective: Uncover a concealed message hidden within a layered radio transmission by analysing its spectrogram.

Abstract

Stellar Frequencies presents a faint, high-frequency signal buried beneath a dominant carrier wave. Solvers must examine frequency bands, filter noise, and reconstruct the masked transmission to extract and decode a drifting cipher embedded beyond the limits of casual listening. The challenge tests skills in signal processing, waveform analysis, and creative problem-solving.

1. File Given

We were given a .wav file that produced a sound similar to beeping.

2. Solution

The first step was to run the file through a DTMF Decoder to check for any encoded tones — this returned nothing useful.

Given the nature of the file, the next logical step was to inspect it visually. Loading the .wav into Sonic Visualiser and switching to the spectrogram view immediately revealed the flag rendered directly in the frequency domain.
